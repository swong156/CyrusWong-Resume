My name is Cyrus Wong. I am a first year Pre-Business student at the University of California, Riverside. In the following years of my education, I plan to take courses such as Economics, Business management, Business ethics, Business law, Communications, Marketing etc. that will equip me with the needed skills to allow me to flourish in the business world. Currently, I am taking courses such as Intro to Computer Science, Pre-Calculus and English 1A that fulfills the prerequisites for me to apply to be in the business program at the end of my sophomore year. When I officially become a part of the business program, I would love to specialize in Marketing. In this day and age, technology allows consumers to browse and shop with ease, with up to thousands or even millions of options, it is important to know how to promote your product and make it stand out from the rest of the competition. With me specializing in marketing, I will be able to learn how to do exactly that. Whether it is pricing a product, distribution strategies,designing or promotion, it will definitely allow me to sell a product in a fast paced environment.

Starting from a young age, I have developed a strong passion for business. My dream of becoming a businessman was heavily inspired and influenced by my father since he owns a business as well. Growing up around him, I was extremely fascinated by the way he deal with various problems that occur within his company on a daily basis. From external affairs such as finding new customers, helping his customers deal witht their problems,to interal affairs such as figuring out ways to lower production cost, making sure the product quality is on point, dealing with employees etc. I am always amazed by the creativity and experience it takes for him to succeed in his business.

As I continue my journey in becoming a businessman, my dream is to one day own a clothing store, regardless if it's my personal brand or as a retailer for other brands. This dream of mine began because of my father as well. He runs a factory in China which does production for a few streetwear brands. Being able to see designers or other celebrities wearing his production made me drawn to the fashion industry. At the beginning, I had hope of becoming a desginer, however, I realize my talent is not at drawing things but rather turning a piece of clothing into a fashionable outfit. Therefore, I strated changing my future goals from being a desginer to owning my fashion brand
